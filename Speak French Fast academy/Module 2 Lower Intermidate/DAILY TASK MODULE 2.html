DAILY TASK MODULE 2

Welcome to Module 2

NOTE: The daily tasks below are designed to help you totally immerse in the language. They do not replace your live classes. I strongly encourage you to let go of any old learning methods you may be used to. Don’t compare—just follow our unique system. It has produced the same extraordinary results that brought you here. Let’s go!

This course is simple: You have 3 daily tasks.

👉 Daily Task 1  – Grammar and Listening comprehension
Lesson 1 (Grammar): Watch 2 lessons (from YouTube or Google Drive) with accompanying classwork.
Then record a 4–10 minute WhatsApp voice note summarizing what you learned:

  • Key lessons
  • New expressions
  • Classwork answers

Note: The higher the quality of your summary, the better your score—so take notes in your SFF Notebook while watching.
Important: Attach the video links + titles in your written report.
Voice note must be recorded directly on WhatsApp, not via a separate app.

Lesson 2: Listening Comprehension: Follow the attached link to do your listening test for the day, take a screenshot of your score and post on your PRP. Do this everyday

Objective: Grammar, Listening, Vocabulary

👉 Daily Task 2 – Reading Comprehension
 Follow the reading instructions for the text of the day.

  • Take a screenshot of the text and your score
  • Post it in your forum
  • Send a WhatsApp voice note reading the text aloud

Objective: Reading, Speaking, Listening

👉 Daily Task 3 – Speaking Practice
 This is your most important task. Complete the speaking task of the day carefully—it’s designed to skyrocket your fluency.

This is not a big deal—just dedicate 2 hours a day.
The first 2–3 days might be tough as you're adjusting, but it gets easier quickly.

NOTE: You’re to complete daily tasks 5 times a week (Monday to Friday).
Saturdays and Sundays are Zoom days for revision and exam prep.
Your report on Zoom days will be based solely on lessons learned during the session.
If your Day 1 falls on a Zoom day, your tasks move to Day 2—Zoom days are task-free.

Deadline for submission: 11:59 PM in your time zone

Now, let’s begin…👇
