🌟 Welcome to Module 5: Put Your French into Practice 🌟

Congratulations on making it this far! 🎉 You have built a strong foundation in French, and now it’s time to take your skills to the next level. In this module, you will refine your language abilities through immersive and dynamic tasks designed to enhance your speaking, listening, reading, and writing proficiency.

The structure remains familiar—Daily Tasks 1 and 2 will continue as before. However, in Task 3, you will further develop your communication skills by engaging in both speaking and writing activities. The speaking practice involves expressing your opinions on various topics, while the writing task will challenge you to translate short texts from English to French, reinforcing vocabulary and grammatical accuracy.

By actively participating in this module, you will strengthen your confidence in real-world communication, ensuring that your French is not just theoretical but practical and fluent. Keep pushing forward—you’re on the path to mastery! 🚀
