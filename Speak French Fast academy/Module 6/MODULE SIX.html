Module 6: From Theory to Practice

Congratulations on your amazing progress! 🎉 You've already built a strong foundation in French, and this module is designed to help you reach the next level. Get ready to refine your skills through practical and stimulating tasks that will transform your knowledge into true fluency.

The module structure will feel familiar: you'll continue with Daily Tasks 1, 2 and 3. The daily task 2 is a listening and reading practice from RFI Savoir. The big change is in Task 3, where you'll develop your ability to communicate both verbally and in writing. You'll learn to express your opinions on various topics and practice translating short texts (from English to French) during the first week. In the following weeks, you'll write an informal letter to solidify what you've learned and improve your precision.

This module is your launchpad toward more confident and fluid communication. It ensures that your French isn't just learned—it's lived. Keep pushing forward: mastery is within your reach! 🚀
