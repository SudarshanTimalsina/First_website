DAILY TASKS 1B

Welcome to Module 1B – Let’s Build Something Great

Learning a foreign language is like building a house. And the most important part of any house is the foundation.

How strong your foundation is will determine how high you can go. So don’t take this module lightly—Module 1B is arguably the most important phase of your French journey.

This is where you begin to solidify the foundation of your French language mansion.

By now, you're no longer a beginner—you’re becoming mature in the language. So your personal principle for this module should be:

“I will not cut corners. I will complete this module with 100% integrity.”

You’ll notice that some lessons from Module 1A have been repeated. That’s intentional. These are foundational topics that require deeper mastery. Approach them with a fresh mindset—as though you're learning them for the first time.

Are you ready?

Let’s goooo!
You’re building something amazing.👇
