Welcome to Speak French Fast Academy – Module 1A

Congratulations and welcome!
Before anything else, I want you to make a strict decision to follow the instructions below for the next one month. If you do, you’ll be shocked by your progress in French.

PLEASE NOTE:
 The daily tasks below are designed to help you completely immerse yourself in the language. They do not replace your Zoom or one on one classes where you are taught live. So it’s important that you kill the mindset of traditional classroom learning. Don’t compare. Don’t analyze.
 Just follow this system that has produced extraordinary results—and it’s the reason you joined.

Your Daily Routine: Just 3 Tasks a Day

You only need to complete 3 focused tasks every day.

👉 Task 1: Grammar and Vocabulary (Lesson Videos)

  • Watch 2 video lessons (from YouTube or Google Drive)
  • Each video comes with short classwork
  • As you watch, take detailed notes in your SFF Notebook
  • When you’re done, record a WhatsApp voice note (minimum 4 mins, max 8 mins) where you:
  • Summarize what you learned from both videos
  • Mention new expressions or key grammar rules
  • Provide answers to your classwork

IMPORTANT:
 Send your voice note directly on WhatsApp (Do not use a recording app).
In your report, also include:

  • Links + Titles of the videos you watched

OBJECTIVE: Grammar | Vocabulary | Writing

👉 Task 2: Reading & Pronunciation Practice

  • Follow the instructions for the reading text of the day
  • Take a screenshot of the text and post it in your forum
  • Send a voice note reading the text aloud with correct pronunciation

OBJECTIVE: Reading | Speaking | Listening

👉 Task 3: Speaking Practice (Basic Conversations)

  • Record a short video of yourself acting out a conversation from the Basic Conversation eBook and Audiobook
  • Create longer conversations everyday
  • A sample video of this task has already been sent to your page

OBJECTIVE: Oral Expression | Fluency

Time Commitment

You only need to dedicate 2 hours per day.
Your first 2 days may feel tough because everything is new—but it gets faster and easier from Day 3 onwards.

Weekly Schedule (IMPORTANT)

You will complete daily tasks 5 days a week.
 Saturdays and Sundays are for Zoom classes, revision, and exam preparation.
On Zoom days:

  • You do not do the 3 daily tasks.  You only do written expression on Saturdays
  • Your report is based only on lessons learned during the Zoom session,  except on Saturdays where you also submit your writing task

NOTE: If your Day 1 falls on a Zoom day, your 3 tasks will start on Day 2.
Each student still completes 5 reporting days every week.

Daily Report Submission Deadline:
 All daily tasks must be submitted before 11:59 PM (your local time zone) each day. Otherwise you'll be asked to restart your cycle.

You’re now ready.
This program works. Trust the process. Follow the system. Watch your French explode.

Let’s begin!
