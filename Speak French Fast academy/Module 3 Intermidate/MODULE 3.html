 🌟 Welcome to Module 3: Elevate Your French Mastery 🌟

Congratulations 🎉 upon making it to Module 3!  You’ve been building a solid foundation, and now it's time to level up. This module will challenge and refine your French language skills in a way that is both immersive and dynamic.

 🎯 What to Expect:

In Module 3, we’ll sharpen your focus on key areas that will take your fluency to new heights:

👉 Gain confidence in using both past and future tenses.

👉 Explore more conjugation moods (like the conditional and subjunctive) and expand into complex tenses.

   

👉 Learn to express yourself more accurately and fluently by diversifying your vocabulary.

   

👉 speaking in real-life scenarios, integrating logical connectors and coordinating conjunctions to create more fluid, natural conversations.

🚀 From this point on, you're stepping into a full French-speaking environment. Every task, from your first daily task, should be conducted entirely in 🇨🇵 French 🇨🇵. 

This is where you truly become comfortable thinking and speaking in French. Your ability to engage confidently with the language will grow rapidly.

Get ready to immerse yourself, challenge your limits, and unlock new layers of your French fluency. The journey is intense but rewarding.

NOTE: ALL YOUR REPORTS AT THIS LEVEL ARE 100% IN FRENCH

What this means is that after watching your two videos in task 1, you are to give an extensive summary (in French) on lessons learnt (in past tense).

Good luck!
